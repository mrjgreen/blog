Having policies in place for the technical cornerstones of your organisation
can prevent "accidental anarchy" within your teams.

<!--more-->

### Cornerstones

 * Documentation
 * Communication
 * Version Control
 * Security
 * Data
 * Application Stack
 * Infrastructure
 * Deployment
 * Logging and Monitoring
